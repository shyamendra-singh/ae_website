Labs

Soil and Water Engineering Lab

This lab allows students to study the properties of soil and water, analyze their interaction, and design solutions for sustainable land and water management practices.

Irrigation Engineering Lab

In this lab, students gain hands-on experience with irrigation systems, design efficient water delivery methods, and learn techniques for optimizing water use in agriculture.

Farm Machinery and Power Lab

This lab equips students with the knowledge and skills to operate, maintain, and troubleshoot various farm machinery used for agricultural production processes.

Post Harvest Engineering Lab

This lab focuses on technologies and practices for handling, processing, storing, and preserving agricultural products after harvest, minimizing losses and ensuring food security.
